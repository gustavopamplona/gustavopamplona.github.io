Research Lines

Here's what I have been researching.

Pushing the limits of neuroscientific knowledge.

Neurofeedback

Using fMRI, I found that it is possible to train large-scale brain networks, which improve attention in the short term and that this ability lasts for at least two months.

Visuomotor integration

I also did quite some work in mapping the visuomotor integration of the brain, using experiments of mental rotation and rubber-hand illusion, for example.

Prediction of reading skills

Building up on my previous research on human intelligence, nowadays I'm focused on predicting reading skills in children using their brain images.
